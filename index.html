My Projects

Beer Raiders

"Beer Raiders" is a Python-based arcade-style game inspired by the classic "Space Invaders." Players take on the role of a determined bar patron defending a beer production factory from mischievous alien spaceships. Armed with a trusty weapon, the player must shoot at the descending extraterrestrial ships, which are bent on pilfering cases of beer from the production line. The player’s objective is to eliminate all alien spaceships before they reach the factory floor, ensuring the beer remains safe and sound. It's a quirky and humorous twist on a retro gaming favorite!

Kind Space

This program is a Facebook-inspired application built with TypeScript and React, designed to foster kindness and positivity. Users can create profiles where they share and post their kind acts, accompanied by pictures and text descriptions. The application sends daily "kind suggestions" to inspire users to perform thoughtful gestures for themselves or others.

A key feature of the app is an interactive map of Albuquerque, NM, which displays pinpoints of locations where users have experienced or performed acts of kindness. These pins create a visual representation of kindness spreading across the community. The app encourages collaboration, positivity, and sharing, making it a platform dedicated to enhancing compassion in everyday life.

T-Shirt Calculator

This application is tailored for users seeking to purchase shirts by offering a streamlined and interactive shopping experience. It allows users to view products categorized by design and style, enabling easier selection. Once a specific shirt style and desired quantity are chosen, the app employs a custom logic to calculate the price based on the shirt's attributes and the selected quantity. Finally, the application presents the total price, ensuring users have a clear understanding of their purchase.

Self Service Menu

This application replicates the functionality of a McDonald’s digital checkout kiosk by providing an online platform for ordering fast food. Users can browse a digital menu showcasing items like burgers, fries, and other products, complete with descriptions and prices. They can select their desired products and quantities, and the application dynamically calculates the total cost in real-time. It offers a seamless and efficient digital ordering experience, mirroring the convenience of a self-service fast food kiosk.
