Purpose: This project is to be used as a professional development tool. I will showcase my achievements in school and personal projects. There will also be a list of professional certificates and educational awards. Having the educational. professional, and personal achievements listed will attract the employers in Tech and IT.

Audience: The site has the intention of attracting professionals within the IT industry. The target individual is a person who is looking to hire for a position within the company. Moreover, I will be happy to attract other developers who are working on projects and are looking for a collaborative partner to contribute to a project. Target audience to include: peers, collaborators, hiring managers, and recruiters.

A secondary purpose is to have other developers reach out to me to comment on my achievements and education. The website will be an excellent addition to my professional portfolio and I will present it to anyone that wants to know more about my professional and educational work/accomplishments.

Sherri is a people person and enjoys spending time with potential investors and relaying messages to her employees. She does not have much time to spend looking at applicants' portfolio sites and will skim through resumes 30 seconds at a time. Her company has become a desirable place for Tech students and graduates to work at. She does not hold people's resume or experience as impressive and rather looks for the ability to create outstanding website content. Sherri has a strong social media presence and attends tech and restaurant community networking events. She has found her employees through events, LinkedIn, and X.

She is frustrated because she has felt that two of the three new hires must be hired within 2 weeks. The interviews are ongoing, but she has not heard of candidates that hold the personality and dedication that is needed to push the company forward onto future success.

Goals: Sherri is focused on filling the role of the web developer to allow for the website to be customized to the specific details that have been laid out by the UI/UX team. The most important thing for the company is to have employees that can maintain company culture and morale. Can Sherri find a placement for a candidate by screening the candidate, over the phone, for 3 minutes max? The time for interviews are extremely limited and must be saved for those who are very much like the desired ideal employee. Can she find the candidate by taking quick glaces through the applicants' personal sites? The job market is very competitive, so there are many candidates that must be screened.

User Story: As a user, I need to quickly find if the candidate holds the necessary traits that will warrant him or her a phone call. There isn't much time to fully ensure the competence of a fully capable web developer by looking at websites, so a coding challenge may also be necessary as a final step.
